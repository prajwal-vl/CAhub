CA Hub — Practice Management, Reimagined

An all-in-one cloud platform built for Chartered Accountants: manage clients, automate tasks, store documents securely, track compliance deadlines and collect payments — all from a single dashboard.

Early access & partner offers for CA firms. Launching in January 2026.

Problem

Chartered Accountants face heavy manual workload, document chaos (WhatsApp/email/physical), compliance overload (GST, ITR, ROC filings), communication gaps and late payments — existing tools are fragmented.

Our Solution

CA Hub is an all-in-one SaaS platform that centralizes client & task management, secure document sharing, automated compliance reminders, in-app communication, invoicing & payment collection, and cloud access so firms can run smoothly.

Market Opportunity

  • ~4 lakh Chartered Accountants in India; accounting services market > ₹20,000 Cr and growing.
  • Global CA market 2.5M+ — digitization demand rising.
  • Target: CA firms, individual CAs, CA students, and SMBs needing accounting services.
